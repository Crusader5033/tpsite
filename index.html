Blue&Gray - American Civil War Strategy Game

About Blue&Gray

Blue&Gray is a strategy game set in the American Civil War era. The basic game loop is inspired by classic Tower Defense games with an RTS twist. Your goal will be to hold out as long as you can to achieve the best score. The RTS twist means you can build troops to defend your base as well as capture vital points on the map to gain strategic advantages like artillery or better logistics. You will also need to generate resources to make troops, earn money, and build buildings and defenses.

Features

  • Simple 2D pixel graphics
  • Strategic gameplay
  • Classic RTS experience
  • Long-term support
  • Authentic music

Tech Stack Used

  • Aseprite - Pixel graphic (models, animations, UI)
  • Unity - Engine
